EaseDisplay

A menu bar tool that lets you use MacBook Pro Retina's highest and unsupported resolutions.
As an example, a Retina MacBook Pro 13" can be set to 3360×2100 maximum resolution, as opposed to Apple's max supported 1680×1050.

Download
Free | Official trial version.

Main Features

    With EaseDisplay you can keep control of your screen resolution and the way, every single app displays its content on your screen.

    For more practical results, add EaseDisplay.app to your Login Items in System Preferences ➡ Users & Groups ➡ Login Items. This way EaseDisplay will run automatically on startup.
